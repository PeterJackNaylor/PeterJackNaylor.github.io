CBIO is a joint research unit involving [Mines ParisTech](http://www.mines-paristech.eu/), [Institut Curie](http://www.institut-curie.org/?prehome=0/) and [INSERM](http://english.inserm.fr/). 
The center is dedicated to bio-informatics and computational systems biology of cancer. 
I am supervised by [Thomas Walter](http://members.cbio.mines-paristech.fr/~twalter/) and my main domain is bio-imaging.
I am very happy to collaborate in any bio-imaging project related (or using) to machine learning and deep learning. Don't hesitate to contact me at: firstname.lastname@mines-paristech.fr.
